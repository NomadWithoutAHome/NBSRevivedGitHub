Our History

"No Bullsh!t Simpsons" is your go-to destination for a hassle-free and mobile-friendly way to enjoy your favorite Simpsons episodes. Our journey began back in 2015 when we realized that finding a simple, ad-minimal, and browser-based platform to watch The Simpsons was harder than it should be.

Who i am

As a passionate programmer, not a professional web designer, I took it upon myself to create a platform that fulfills this need. Our website was born out of a desire for convenience and simplicity. We understand that your time is valuable, and that's why we've strived to make your Simpsons viewing experience as straightforward as possible.

Our Commitment

Our commitment is to provide you with a clean, efficient, and ad-light platform for enjoying The Simpsons from your browser. While we do our best to design the site with user-friendliness in mind, please bear with us as we continue to refine and improve. Some sections of the site may still be under construction, and your feedback is invaluable in helping us grow.

Get Involved

We encourage you to get involved and help us make "No Bullsh!t Simpsons" even better. If you encounter any bugs, quirks, or issues while using our platform, please don't hesitate to reach out. Join our IRC channel to report any problems or simply to chat with fellow Simpsons enthusiasts. Your input plays a crucial role in enhancing the user experience for everyone.

Thank you for being a part of our community, and we hope you enjoy your time here at "No Bullsh!t Simpsons." Happy watching!
